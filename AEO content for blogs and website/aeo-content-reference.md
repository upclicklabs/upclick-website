## Key Statistics to Use

### The Problem (for Hero/Problem sections)
- **95%** of B2B buyers plan to use generative AI in their buying process
- **93%** of marketing leaders say AEO will be critical to success in next 2 years
- **Only 25%** of marketing practitioners fully understand what AEO is
- **15-25%** drop in SEO traffic across the board (Bain)
- AI search projected to surpass traditional search in just **3 years** (SEMrush)

### Content Freshness
- **95%** of ChatGPT citations point to pages updated in the last 10 months
- Webflow saw **42% more traffic** and **14% more signups** from content refreshes in under 2 months
- **5x** more refreshed content = **40%** lift in visits

### Technical AEO
- **73%** of page-one results use schema markup
- **88%** of sites don't use schema at all (huge opportunity!)
- **485%** QoQ growth in LLM-attributed signups from technical optimizations

### Business Impact
- **8%** of total new signups came directly from LLMs
- AI signups = **42%** of total non-brand organic signups
- **+24%** new signups year-over-year when combining AI + non-brand SEO

## The AEO Maturity Model (4 Categories)

### 1. CONTENT
**Foundation of AEO - delivering clear, comprehensive answers AI can understand**

| Level | Name | Focus |
|-------|------|-------|
| 1 | Keywords | On-page keyword SEO → shift to answering real buyer questions |
| 2 | Answers | Start answering questions, featured snippets, content freshness |
| 3 | Structure | Organize question clusters, cornerstone content, competitor comparisons |
| 4 | Pillar | Establish hierarchy, funnel-spanning content, localization |
| 5 | Authority | Programmatic AEO, personalized content, industry-defining assets |

**Key Actions:**
- Add FAQs, tables of contents, and summary snippets
- Refresh content to be up-to-date (within 10 months)
- Create cornerstone pieces (annual reports, research studies)
- Use question-based headings
- Improve readability (Flesch score 80+, ideally 90+)

---

### 2. TECHNICAL
**The backbone enabling content to be discovered and interpreted by AI**

| Level | Name | Focus |
|-------|------|-------|
| 1 | On-page SEO | Keywords, crawlability, metadata, HTTPS |
| 2 | Page Structure | Schema markup, heading hierarchy, accessibility |
| 3 | Speed & Consistency | Site performance, nested schema, IndexNow |
| 4 | Automation | Automated schema, redirects for hallucinated URLs |
| 5 | Next-Gen Standards | llms.txt, MCP servers, emerging AI protocols |

**Key Actions:**
- Add schema markup (75%+ sitewide coverage goal)
- Use clear heading hierarchy (H1→H2→H3)
- Implement IndexNow for Bing (used by ChatGPT)
- Create llms.txt file for AI crawlers
- Redirect hallucinated/mistyped URLs from AI referrals
- Maintain ~100% crawlability, 95%+ accessibility

---

### 3. AUTHORITY
**Credibility built through consistent visibility and trust signals**

| Level | Name | Focus |
|-------|------|-------|
| 1 | Backlinks | Traditional link building + E-E-A-T signals |
| 2 | New Channels | Digital PR, podcasts, Reddit, Quora engagement |
| 3 | Digital PR | Thought leadership, visual storytelling, Wikipedia |
| 4 | Visual Storytelling | Engaging experiences, citation management |
| 5 | Broad Recognition | Industry-defining, widespread positive citations |

**E-E-A-T Framework:**
- **Experience:** Highlight hands-on experience, real case studies
- **Expertise:** Demonstrate depth, feature credentialed voices
- **Authoritativeness:** Earn organic mentions across respected platforms
- **Trustworthiness:** Cite reliable data, clear sources, transparent authorship

**Key Actions:**
- Add author bios to key content
- Claim Google Knowledge Panel
- Create/update Wikipedia page
- Engage authentically on Reddit, Quora
- Pursue podcasts, webinars, guest posts
- Invest in visual storytelling (infographics, animations, video)

---

### 4. MEASUREMENT
**Connecting visibility to impact**

| Level | Name | Focus |
|-------|------|-------|
| 1 | Keywords | Track target keyword rankings |
| 2 | Mentions | Manual monitoring of AI mentions |
| 3 | Benchmarks | Share of voice, accuracy tracking |
| 4 | Real-time | Integrated feedback loops |
| 5 | Predictive | Anticipate trends, rapid response |

**Key Metrics to Track:**
- **Inclusion:** Is your brand included in AI output?
- **Citation:** Are your owned resources linked?
- **Prominence:** How early/prominently does brand appear?
- **Sentiment:** Positive, neutral, or negative?
- **Share of Voice:** Frequency vs competitors
- **Accuracy:** Is the information correct and current?

## Assessment Tool Categories (for your tool)

### 1. Content Score (30% weight)
- FAQ sections present
- How-to/guide content
- Question-based headings
- Content freshness (updated within 10 months)
- Answer completeness
- Readability (Flesch score)

### 2. Technical Score (25% weight)
- Schema.org markup coverage
- Meta descriptions
- Heading structure (H1-H6)
- Page speed
- Mobile-friendly
- HTTPS/security
- Sitemap with last modified dates

### 3. Authority Score (25% weight)
- Author attribution/bios
- About page quality
- Brand mentions across web
- E-E-A-T signals
- Citations/sources
- Wikipedia presence
- Google Knowledge Panel

### 4. Measurement Score (20% weight)
- Analytics present
- Goal tracking setup
- AI referrer detection capability
- Conversion tracking
